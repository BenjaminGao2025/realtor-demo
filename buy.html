Buying a Home in Vancouver's Westside

From pre-approval to closing day, Demo guides you through every step of buying your dream home.

Your Buying Journey

8 Steps to Your New Home

A clear, proven process that makes buying a home in Vancouver's Westside as smooth as possible.

1

Mortgage Pre-Approval

Get pre-approved to know your budget and show sellers you're serious. Canadian residents and foreign buyers have different requirements.

How Demo helps: "I connect you with trusted mortgage brokers who specialize in Vancouver Westside properties and work with both local and international clients."
2

Define Your Search

Identify your must-haves: neighborhood, property type, size, budget, school catchment, and commute needs.

How Demo helps: "We'll have a detailed discovery call to understand exactly what you're looking for, then I'll set up customized alerts."
3

Market Research

Understand current market conditions, recent comparable sales, and pricing trends in your target neighborhoods.

How Demo helps: "I provide a comprehensive market analysis with historical data, price trends, and forecasts for UBC, Point Grey, and Dunbar."
4

Property Search & Viewings

Tour shortlisted properties, evaluate condition, potential, and value. Attend open houses and private viewings.

How Demo helps: "I'll identify properties before they hit the market through my network, and provide expert insight during every viewing."
5

Making an Offer

Submit a competitive offer with appropriate subjects (financing, inspection, title review). Strategy matters in competitive markets.

How Demo helps: "I craft strategic offers based on market data, comparable sales, and my experience with local sellers' expectations."
6

Home Inspection & Due Diligence

Professional inspection, title search, strata document review (for condos/townhouses), and any additional assessments.

How Demo helps: "I coordinate all inspections and review strata documents with you, highlighting any red flags or concerns."
7

Financing & Legal

Finalize mortgage, work with your lawyer/notary, review and sign documents, arrange insurance.

How Demo helps: "I stay in constant communication with your mortgage broker and lawyer to ensure everything stays on track."
8

Closing & Possession

Complete the transaction, receive keys, and move into your new home. In BC, closing typically takes 30–60 days from accepted offer.

How Demo helps: "I'm with you until the keys are in your hand — and beyond. I'll help you settle into your new neighborhood."

First-Time Home Buyer?

British Columbia offers several programs to help first-time buyers get into the market. You may qualify for:

  • Property Transfer Tax Exemption — Up to $500K exempt, reduced rate up to $835K for qualifying first-time buyers
  • First Home Savings Account (FHSA) — Tax-free savings account designed to help you save for your first home
  • Home Buyers' Plan (HBP) — Withdraw up to $60,000 from your RRSP tax-free to buy your first home
